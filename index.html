About

I'm finishing my Bachelor's in Mechatronics Engineering in Berlin, currently near the top of my class. I'm happiest where hardware, software, and AI meet, the point where code starts moving things in the real world.

What I enjoy most is the part after the build: explaining it. I've spent years translating between technical and non-technical people, from bridging engineers and new trainees on the shop floor, to being the public spokesperson for a Special Olympics team, to becoming the top-selling associate across Smartech's European stores by making technology genuinely click for people.

Right now I'm going deep on AI-native search, vector databases, RAG, and open-source AI, and looking for ways to turn what I learn into clear writing, sharp visuals, and example projects. I don't claim to know everything yet, and that's exactly why I want to grow as a developer advocate: building in public and learning out loud.
